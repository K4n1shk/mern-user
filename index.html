Program Information

  • IIITDM Kurnool is an Institute of National Importance established in 2014 and fully funded by MHRD, Govt. of India with the mandate of specializing in design and manufacturing oriented engineering education and research.
  • The curricula and other academic/research activities adopt an inter-disciplinary approach with a focus on experiential learning and information science-based modern tools and techniques.

About OAMS

Mission

To become a center of excellence pioneering in education, research & development, and leaders in design & manufacturing.

Vision

To become a leading institute of higher learning in Information Technology enabled design & manufacturing to create technologies and technologists befitting the industries globally.

Charter

To carry out advanced research and development activities in design and manufacturing technologies, both on its own and on sponsorship basis for the industry.
